Modular Construction

Here at Adapt Engineering, we pride ourselves on producing innovative, but high quality designs. Our UK team, based in Edinburgh, were recently involved in one of the first major modular construction builds – the Holiday Inn Express project in Manchester. Each bedroom of this 220-bed hotel was a separate volumetric unit, and because of this, construction time was significantly reduced, with all bedroom placed on site within 3 weeks.

We are particularly proud of achieving the “Retail/Leisure Project of the Year” at the 2018 Offsite Construction Awards, and this is testament to our hard working staff, who will go above and beyond in order to help you realise your ideal design.

Vehicle-free streets

Adapt Engineering provide best in class design support for a variety of clients across the public and private sectors. We have won several recent accolades for our work in developing new sustainable travel networks and for integrating new accessible streetscapes into historic environments.

Our recent work on Edinburgh’s George Street, located in the heart of the World Heritage Area, has recently been selected by the City of Edinburgh Council (CEC) in a design competition. The design includes creating a vehicle free streetscape, creation of active travel connections, development of the “Café Culture” landscaping, diversion and upgrades to underground services and resurfacing to provide a high quality surface to match the unique nature of the New Town.

Western Harbour

The proposed community development is a constituent part of the broader programme to regenerate Leith. Leith's redevelopment is one of the largest regeneration schemes in the UK, affecting Leith Docks and the surrounding area.

To ensure the continued success of the regeneration of Leith, our design is specially catered to current and future residents of Leith, promoting communitarian and social values. However, we also take special care to preserve the industrial heritage of the area, championing Leith's proud past.

Edinburgh Haymarket

Adapt Engineering is known for its passion and expertise to deliver technically excellent solutions for rail, road, buildings, and land remediation. Our recent project for the Haymarket site in Edinburgh is evidence of the technical abilities of our geotechnical team.

The site in the centre of Edinburgh remained undeveloped due to shallow Victorian rail tunnels running below the ground. Adapt Engineering designed and supervised piling activities on both sides of the tunnel as well as 6m reduction of ground level to allow entrance to Quartermile Developments from the street level.

The works for this challenging development included a detailed investigation of the tunnels, local grouting, and local sprayed concrete lining. Real-time data was used for finite element modelling during construction.
